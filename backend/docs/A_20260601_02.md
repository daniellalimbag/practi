# Professional conduct during your internship

Treat your practicum like a job interview that lasts weeks: arrive on time, dress to the workplace norm, and communicate delays as soon as you know them.

Use company-approved channels for sensitive topics. Do not share proprietary data, credentials, or customer information outside approved tools.

When you finish a task, ask your supervisor what would help next. Proactive communication builds trust and often leads to more interesting assignments.

If you make a mistake, own it quickly, explain what happened at a factual level, and propose a fix or ask for guidance. Teams value accountability over perfection.
