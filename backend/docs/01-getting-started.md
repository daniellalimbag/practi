# Getting started on your practicum

Welcome to your practicum or internship. Before day one, confirm your start date, reporting location, and primary supervisor with your school coordinator and the host organization.

Bring a notebook or digital log to capture daily tasks, questions, and skills you practice. Many programs require a weekly reflection; starting the habit early makes final reports easier.

If paperwork is unclear (background checks, NDAs, or school forms), ask early rather than the morning you are due on site.
